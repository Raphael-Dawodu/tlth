Meet the Founder

Dr Priscilla Ogunlude, PhD

Founder, The Learning Treehouse

Hi, I’m Dr. Priscilla Ogunlude, founder of The Learning Treehouse.

With a PhD in Chemical Engineering and nearly a decade of experience in Teaching, Research, and the Oil and Gas Industry. I have worked across various levels of education—from university lecture halls to primary school classrooms. My greatest passion lies in helping younger learners build the solid foundation they need to take them through the higher education system and unlock their full potential.

Over the years, I’ve consistently supported children in learning, combining my academic expertise with a nurturing approach that builds confidence, curiosity, and a love for learning. As a mum of two, I also understand the challenges and expectations of the UK school system from both a parent’s and educator’s perspective.

At The Learning Treehouse, my vision is simple: to provide high-quality, affordable tuition that empowers children to thrive—academically and personally. Every child deserves the opportunity to succeed, and we're here to make that happen.
