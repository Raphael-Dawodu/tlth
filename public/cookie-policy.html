Cookie Policy

Effective date: 17 April 2026

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience and help us deliver personalised content.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and provide essential functionality.

2. How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies: Necessary to run the website, support navigation and secure access.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website, which helps us improve content.
  • Functional cookies: Remember preferences such as region, accessibility settings or progress through forms.

3. Third-party cookies

We may use third-party services like Google Analytics to collect anonymised data about visitor behaviour. These cookies are controlled by the third party and can be managed directly through their privacy settings.

4. Cookie consent

By using our website, you agree to our use of cookies described in this policy. If you want to limit or block cookies, you can do so through your browser settings.

5. Managing cookies

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete cookies, or block cookies from specific websites.

6. Data protection

This Cookie Policy forms part of our wider Privacy Policy, which explains how we collect, use and protect personal information.

7. Contact

If you have questions about cookies or how we use them, please contact us at contactus@thelearningtreehouse.co.uk.
